</p><p>Airplane deicing fluids are specialized chemical solutions designed to remove or prevent the formation of ice on aircraft surfaces. These fluids play a critical role in ensuring flight safety by preventing ice accumulation on wings, fuselage, and other critical areas that can compromise aerodynamic performance. Deicing fluids are typically composed of glycol-based compounds mixed with water and corrosion inhibitors, providing both deicing and anti-icing properties.</p><p>
