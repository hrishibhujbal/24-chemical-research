<h1>Flexible Polyurethane Foams Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><p>Flexible polyurethane foams (FPF) are lightweight, durable, and versatile materials used in a wide range of applications, including furniture, automotive seating, mattresses, and packaging. These foams are primarily produced from the reaction of polyols and isocyanates, resulting in open-cell structures that provide cushioning, resilience, and insulation properties. Due to their flexibility, these foams have become essential in industries such as healthcare, construction, and electronics. The increasing demand for comfort and energy-efficient materials has further propelled their adoption in various sectors.</p><p>
