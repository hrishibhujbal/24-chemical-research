<strong>Challenges</strong></p><p>
</p><p><strong>Logistical Constraints:</strong> Transportation and storage of slag, due to its bulk and weight, pose logistical challenges.</p><p><strong>Market Awareness:</strong> Limited awareness among stakeholders about the benefits of slag in certain regions restricts its adoption.</p><p>
