</p><p>
</p><p>Fluoropolymer lining sheets are specialized sheets made from fluoropolymer materials such as PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene) and similar compounds. These sheets are widely utilized to line tanks, pipes, and industrial equipment, providing superior chemical resistance, non-stick properties, and high thermal stability. Their applications span across industries such as chemicals, pharmaceuticals, food processing, and semiconductor manufacturing, ensuring prolonged equipment lifespan and operational efficiency.</p><p>
