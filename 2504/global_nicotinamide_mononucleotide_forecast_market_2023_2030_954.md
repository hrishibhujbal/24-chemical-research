<h1>Nicotinamide Mononucleotide (NMN) Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><p>Beta-Nicotinamide Mononucleotide (NMN) is a naturally occurring nucleotide derived from ribose and nicotinamide. NMN has gained significant attention in the field of anti-aging and longevity research due to its role in promoting healthy aging and mitigating age-related diseases such as diabetes, neurodegenerative disorders, and cardiovascular diseases.</p><p>
