<strong>Market Size</strong></p><p>

It is a crucial fine chemical product and synthetic intermediate widely used in food emulsifiers, cosmetics, pharmaceuticals, and industrial applications. Due to its biodegradable and non-toxic properties, polyglycerol plays a significant role in sustainable and eco-friendly formulations across various industries.</p><p>
