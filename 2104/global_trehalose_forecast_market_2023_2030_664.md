<h1>Trehalose Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><p>Trehalose (C12H22O11) is a naturally occurring disaccharide composed of two glucose molecules linked by an alpha, alpha-1,1-glycosidic bond. It is commonly found in a variety of organisms, including fungi, bacteria, plants, and insects. Trehalose has gained prominence in industries such as food and beverages, pharmaceuticals, cosmetics, and chemicals due to its unique properties, such as high water retention, antioxidant activity, and stability under extreme conditions.</p><p>
