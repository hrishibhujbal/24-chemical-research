<h1>Polydicyclopentadiene (PDCPD) Market, Global Outlook &amp; Forecast 2025-2032</h1><p>Polydicyclopentadiene (PDCPD) is a highly durable thermoset polymer derived from dicyclopentadiene (DCPD) through ring-opening metathesis polymerization (ROMP). It is known for its high impact resistance, chemical resistance, and excellent mechanical properties. PDCPD is widely used in applications requiring robust, lightweight, and chemically resistant materials, such as automotive parts, agricultural machinery, construction materials, and anti-corrosion coatings.</p><p>
