</p><p>Dopant materials are substances introduced into semiconductors, insulators, or other materials to modify their electrical, optical, or structural properties. These materials play a crucial role in industries such as electronics, photovoltaics, and nanotechnology. Common dopant materials include elements like boron, phosphorus, arsenic, and gallium, which are widely used in semiconductor fabrication to enhance conductivity and performance.</p><p>
